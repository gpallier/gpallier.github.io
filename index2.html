Presentation

I am a post-doctoral researcher in the Mathematics department of the university of Fribourg (Switzerland), under the supervision of Prof. Enrico Le Donne. I defended a PhD thesis in 2019, under supervision of Prof. Pierre Pansu, in the Université Paris-Saclay (Orsay, France), and worked as a postdoc in the Università di Pisa, Italy.

Here is an academic curriculum vitae: 

  • in English. 
  • en français.

Research interests: Metric geometry. Geometric group theory. Large-scale geometry, especially of Lie groups. Negative and non-positive curvature. Nilpotent groups. Dehn functions. Analysis on metric spaces. Geometric measure theory.

Links: ORCID, Mathscinet (requires subscription), math genealogy (and a more recent family picture), ResearchGate.
